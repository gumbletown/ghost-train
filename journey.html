Introduction to the Ghost Train

Ghost Train is an immersive and experiential ride playing on both virtual and physical reality. It is curated by artists to be a multi-sensory, virtual reality experience.

There are a total of five carriages on the ride seating 2 adults or 3 children, and depart every 20 seconds. Each passenger will enter the ride via a train carriage wearing an Oculus Rift Headset.

The carriage enters rooms and hallways in a constructed facility which the animation mirrors real time. There will be wind and scent machines in several of the rooms.

The ride is housed and stored in a trailer that attaches to a prime mover for transport, and is highly self sufficient and easy to assemble as a package..
